About Me

Welcome! My name is Kimmy Joe and I am a photographer and coder.

I was born and raised in Suriname, South America where I found my love for photography at the age of fourteen. When I moved to Florida at sixteen one of my biggest obstacles was the language barrier along with the cultural difference. Photography was one way to easily connect with people while allowing me to explore my creative mind.

I attended Florida State University where I fell in love with computer programming. I have since been learning and researching about coding, both front and back end.

My goal is to implement my creativity to combine both photography and coding together.

On this website you will find links to my portfolio for both my photography and computer programming.
